Tools and Methods for Trenchless Pipe Repair

Tools & Equipment

Sewer Camera

Used for inspecting the interior of pipes to identify damage and blockages accurately.

Hydro Jetter

Clears stubborn clogs and prepares pipes for trenchless repairs by removing debris.

Pipe Locator

Helps us accurately locate pipes underground to avoid unnecessary digging.

Trenchless Equipment

Specialized tools used for relining or bursting damaged pipes without excavation.

Methods & Standards

Pipe Relining

This method involves inserting a new lining into the existing pipe, effectively sealing leaks and restoring flow.

Pipe Bursting

Involves breaking apart the damaged pipe while simultaneously installing a new pipe in its place.

Cured-in-Place Pipe (CIPP)

A trenchless method where a resin-saturated liner is inserted into the pipe and cured, creating a new pipe within.
